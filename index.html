            Hello, this is an app!

            Tap the view_headline in the upper-left, then tap one of the buttons at the bottom to create a new list! From there you can add items, delete them, sort them, whatever!

            Checklists behave like, well, checklists! Notes Lists are collections of notes, which can be arranged as tiles or in a list. Notes can have a little bit of styling applied to them to make them **bold** or *italic* or even ~~strikethrough'd~~.

            I suppose it'd be polite of me to add, like, more instructions or whatever, but, counterpoint: I'm lazy.
